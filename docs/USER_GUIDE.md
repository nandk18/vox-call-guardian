# Vox — User Guide

## What is Vox?
Vox is an AI phone receptionist for Indian small businesses. It answers calls 24/7, understands what callers need, and sends you a summary instantly.

## Getting Started
1. Sign up at vox-call-guardian.lovable.app
2. Complete the 3-step onboarding:
   - Enter your business details
   - Review your business information
   - Get your Vox phone number
3. Share your Vox number with customers OR forward your existing number to it

## Features

### Inbox
- See all calls in real time
- Each call shows: caller number, duration, outcome, AI summary
- Full transcript of every call
- Call back, WhatsApp, or resend summary from the inbox

### Your Agent
- Customize your greeting message
- Choose male or female voice
- Select your primary language (Hindi, Tamil, Telugu + 8 more)
- Add your business knowledge: services, hours, FAQs, address
- Click "Rebuild Agent" after changing language or voice to apply changes

### Integrations
- Connect Cal.com to enable live appointment booking during calls
- Google Sheets integration coming soon
- Zapier integration coming soon

### Settings
- Manage notification preferences (Email, WhatsApp, SMS)
- View your Vox number
- Connect your existing number via call forwarding
- Manage your subscription plan

## Notification Channels
- Email: Sent after every call
- SMS: Sent after every call (requires DLT registration in India)
- WhatsApp: Coming soon (requires approved templates)

## Call Forwarding Setup
To forward your existing number to Vox:
- Airtel: *67*+91[VoxNumber]#
- Jio: **67*+91[VoxNumber]#
- BSNL: **61*+91[VoxNumber]#
- Vi: *004*+91[VoxNumber]#

## Language Support
Vox speaks: Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Marathi, Bengali, Gujarati, Punjabi, Odia, English

## FAQ
Q: Will Vox work with my existing number?
A: Yes — use call forwarding codes above.

Q: What happens if I miss a call?
A: Vox answers, collects details, and sends you a summary instantly.

Q: How do I change my business info?
A: Go to Agent page → Knowledge section → edit any field → it saves automatically.

Q: What does Rebuild Agent do?
A: It recreates your AI agent on Bolna with updated language/voice settings. Required after changing language or voice.
